Colombian Arepas

Description

In Colombia we have tons of typical, delicious food; it is something we are proudly known for. But if I could choose the most versatile and delicious thing, it would definitely be arepas. Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of white or yellow cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 talespoon of butter (opcional)
  • 1/2 cup of cheese (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Mix all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl until you have soft dough, then take a little bit and start making the round, flat shape.
    2. Fry in a pan (five minutes each side) or bake in an oven for about ten minutes.
